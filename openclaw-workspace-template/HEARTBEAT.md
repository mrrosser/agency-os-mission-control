Daily Cadence (Revenue Sprint)
- Morning: review lead pipeline, draft outreach, schedule calls.
- Midday: prepare quotes, deposits, and booking follow-ups (drafts only).
- Afternoon: delivery planning and documentation updates.
- End of day: summarize progress and create next-day plan.

Weekly Cadence
- Curate MEMORY.md from daily logs.
- Review pricing/services menu for clarity.
- Tighten allowlists and remove stale access.
