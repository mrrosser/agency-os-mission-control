# Lead Comms Email Shared Voice

## When to use
- The reply should sound like Marcus.
- The thread needs a practical next step rather than generic sales language.

## Workflow
1. Keep tone warm, direct, and practical.
2. Use plain text only.
3. Keep the reply short and move to one clear next step.
4. Avoid hype, robotic lists, and canned closers.

## Verification
- [ ] Tone is human and concise.
- [ ] Plain text only.
- [ ] The reply ends with one clear next step.

## Example prompts
- "Apply lead-comms-email-shared-voice to this outbound draft."
- "Use lead-comms-email-shared-voice and keep the reply direct and plain-spoken."
